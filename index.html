Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nmap and why is it used?
Nmap (Network Mapper) is a free, open-source utility used for network discovery and security auditing by cybersecurity professionals.
Is it legal to use Nmap to scan networks?
Yes, but only on networks you own or have explicit written permission to scan. Unauthorized scanning is illegal.
